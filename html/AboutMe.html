About Me

Hello! My name is Keith Roberts, and I hope you are enjoying my website. I had a lot of fun creating this site, and it makes me happy when others get to see and experience something I have created. Anyway, here is some interesting stuff about me. I was born in Bozeman, Montana, in the year 2002. And I lived there until I was eight years old, when my family decided to pack up and move to Ottumwa, Iowa, where I live now. I attended Ottumwa High School and graduated in 2021. After graduating, I had no idea what I wanted to be. I always had multiple passions in life, but I was always afraid to just pick one. But I can't live in a purgatory of choices forever, so one day I thought, You know what? Ive always enjoyed playing video games, and Ive always been more interested in creating a video game. I have even created a few smaller, unpublished games in the past for fun, so why don't I give it a shot? Why not try to create games for a living? And that led me to join Indian Hills Community College, where I am currently studying to become a software engineer and to give myself enough experience to eventually move on into the gaming industry!
