My Pycharm Pumpkin Project

Here is my pycharm pumpkin project. The goal of this project was to take lines of data from one file and convert it into data that anyone could read on another file.

Lines of data

As you can see here, we have a file with the names of stores along with long lines of numbers. Surprisingly, this data actually means something, and it's my program's job to convert this data into something anybody could read.

The Program

Here is a snippet of just a small portion of my program. The job of this program was to read the data from the original file and convert it into readable data. My program was able to take the first letter of this data and match it to its counterpart. It then takes the numbers, the first three being how many pumpkins each store bought, and the last three numbers being the price they bought the pumpkins for, and again converts this data onto a new file.

The Outcome

And here is the outcome: as you can see, my program converted the old data into something easy for anyone to understand. The new data shows the store name, the location, the amount of pumpkins each store bought, the price they spent per pumpkin, and the total amount spent on pumpkins. My program also shows the subtotals of each company and the grand totals of all the stores combined. I'm pretty proud of this program, which was one of the first major programs I ever built.
